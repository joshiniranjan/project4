Connectors available for all major platforms

Using a simple plug-in asda das will analyse and import your product catalogue, always staying up to date.

All plug-ins for asda das are downloadable either through the asda das configuration proces or can be found on the App store of your chosen platform.

asda das plug-ins are verified by the platform vendor, secure and are read-only.

asda das stays up to date.
24/7/365

Our integrations make sure we always have the latest product information from your store.

This way all prices, product descriptions, images and meta-data stay up-to-date, ensuring your ads are as relevant as possible.

The update frequency of the asda das integration can easily configured dependant on your requirements.

Custom systems fully supported

For custom systems we support a product feed, Google Structured Data, Schema or just by crawling your website.

If you are running a custom shop, or a standard platform that we do not yet support with plugin, either follow our guide for connecting with xml, or drop us a line for a assistance.
